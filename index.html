Our Commitment to You

100% of Your Donation
Goes to the Cause

We made a promise — every dollar you donate goes directly to the community we're serving. Not one cent to salaries, rent, or administration.

M1U's operating costs are funded separately through strategic partnerships, sponsorships, and organizational support — so your generosity goes exactly where it's needed, every single time.

"Most nonprofits ask: will you donate?
M1U asks a different question:
Will you help build a stronger Ummah?"
💰

Your Donations

100% goes directly to featured community campaigns — prayer spaces, masjid support, and community programs.

🤝

Our Operations

Funded separately through partnerships, sponsors, and organizational contributions — never from public donations.

Full Transparency

Impact reports published monthly. Every dollar is accounted for and reported back to our community.

Your Trust is Sacred

We treat every donation as an amanah — a trust — and honor it with complete accountability.
