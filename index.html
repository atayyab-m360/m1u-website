Our Mission

"The Ummah is strongest when we work together — not as separate organizations, but as one community united around a shared purpose."

Our Approach

We Don't Compete.
We Connect.

M1U serves as a bridge between people, mosques, nonprofits, and businesses — creating partnerships that multiply impact instead of dividing it.

🍽️

Feeding the Hungry

Providing meals and food assistance while creating opportunities for compassion, service, and community involvement.

📿

Supporting Our Imams

Strengthening the leaders who guide our communities through education, collaboration, resources, and long-term support.

🤝

Community Support

Helping individuals, families, and local communities through partnerships, volunteerism, and programs that strengthen the Ummah.

💡

Innovation

Using technology and strategic partnerships to connect communities, simplify giving, and maximize impact.

🕌

Mosque Partnerships

Working directly with masjids across the country to serve their congregations and amplify their community impact.

🌍

Global Reach

Building a connected Muslim community where every individual and organization can create lasting impact locally and globally.
